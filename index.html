About Me

我相信，解决复杂争议的深度，取决于知识系统的广度。

作为一名具备系统性建筑学背景的专业人士，我目前的战略重心是构建跨学科的底层逻辑。从物理空间的建造逻辑，到科技与人工智能的演进，再到跨文化的语言沟通，这些看似分散的领域，最终都将成为我深耕国际建筑法律行业的锋利工具。

这是一个持续生长的数字展厅，记录了我从工程实践向法律合规领域的跨越之路。
I believe the depth of resolving complex disputes is determined by the breadth of one's knowledge system.

With a rigorous background in architecture, my current strategic focus is on building an interdisciplinary foundation. From the logic of physical construction to the evolution of AI, and cross-cultural communication—these diverse fields will serve as sharp tools for my future practice in international construction law.

This digital portfolio records my ongoing trajectory from engineering practice to legal compliance.
